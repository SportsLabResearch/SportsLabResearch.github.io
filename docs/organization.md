# Organization

## About SportsLabResearch

SportsLabResearch is an open scientific platform focused on the development of reproducible software, scientific methodologies and technological solutions for sport and health sciences.

The platform integrates scientific research, software development, documentation and knowledge transfer into a unified ecosystem that supports researchers, practitioners and educators.

---

# Mission

To develop open scientific software and reproducible methodologies that facilitate research, teaching and professional practice in sport and health sciences.

---

# Vision

To become an international reference in open scientific software for sport and health sciences through innovation, scientific quality and collaboration.

---
