# About SportsLabResearch

<div class="slr-page-intro">
SportsLabResearch is an international research software initiative focused on sport science, exercise, health and human performance.
</div>

## Purpose

Researchers and professionals frequently depend on fragmented tools, opaque calculations and workflows that are difficult to reproduce. SportsLabResearch addresses this gap by creating a coherent ecosystem of software, documentation, validation and reusable research methods.

## Vision

To become a reference platform for open scientific software and validated digital technologies applied to sport science, exercise and health.

## Mission

<div class="slr-card-grid slr-card-grid--two">
  <article class="slr-card"><h3>Develop</h3><p>Create robust and maintainable research software around real scientific needs.</p></article>
  <article class="slr-card"><h3>Validate</h3><p>Document technical behaviour, analytical decisions and scientific validity.</p></article>

</div>

## Scope

- Wearables and sensor technology.
- Heart rate and heart rate variability.
- GNSS, local positioning and inertial measurement units.
- Sports analytics and performance analysis.
- Artificial intelligence and research data processing.
- Mobile health and remote monitoring.
- Scientific software validation.
- Open educational and reproducible research resources.

## Identity

SportsLabResearch is not a collection of isolated repositories. It is a shared methodological and technical framework in which every project follows common principles for structure, documentation, quality, validation and dissemination.
