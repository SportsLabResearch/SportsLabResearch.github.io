# About SportsLabResearch

SportsLabResearch is an independent research initiative focused on the development of scientific software, hardware and digital technologies for Sports Science.

The project was created to establish a unified ecosystem where research software, educational resources, hardware developments and scientific documentation share the same quality standards, development methodology and visual identity.

---

# Mission

Develop robust, reliable and reproducible scientific software following international research software engineering standards.

---

# Vision

To become an international reference in research software for Sport Sciences through innovation, scientific quality and open science.

---

# Core Principles

* Scientific Excellence
* Reproducible Research
* Open Science
* FAIR Principles
* Software Quality
* Continuous Validation
* Long-Term Sustainability

---

# Main Research Lines

## Research Software

Development of scientific applications for research and professional practice.

## Artificial Intelligence

Machine learning, computer vision and intelligent decision support systems.

## Wearable Technologies

Heart rate variability, physiological monitoring, GPS, IMU and sports sensors.

## Sports Analytics

Performance analysis, data science and predictive analytics.

## Arduino & Embedded Systems

Educational and research platforms based on open hardware.

## Scientific Documentation

Professional documentation using MkDocs and GitHub.
